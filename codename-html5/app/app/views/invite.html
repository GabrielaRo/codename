We are a social, independant and proactive bunch who believe in...

Remote working

We hate offices and want to choose for ourselves which environements inspire and allow us to be most productive.

The empowering impact of technology

The internet has given us an opportunity to redefine the way we work - we out to explore which methods work best for us.

Work putting humans first

Our wellbeing and health shouldn’t be compromised. We’re in search of a better work/life balance and living life.

Supporting each other

Going independant can be a big step and like all things, big decisions are made easier with support from people who understand.
