Do you work remotely and have you ever...

Spent all day working alone and wished you had someone to share a coffee break or grab lunch with?

Needed recommendations on where to work, from great coffee shops to choosing your next nomad location?

Missed having someone to brainstorm with or run an idea past?

Arrived in a new city and wished you could find other nomads, local freelancers or entrepreneurs?

Wished you could ask someone in a similar position for advice because most of your friends don’t work remotely?

Wondered how other location independents do it and wish you could do the same?

So have we, which is why we’ve created this community. We think we can learn so much from each other and that by supporting one other we can better redefine the way in which we choose to live and work.
